The Legend of Marfusha

The first rabbit in space was Marfusha, also known as "Little Martha". She was launched into space on July 2, 1959, aboard a Soviet R2-A rocket ship, along with two dogs, Otvazhnaya (Brave) and Snezhinka (Snowflake), and other animals like rats, mice, and fruit flies.

Marfusha survived the sub-orbital flight and returned safely to Earth. Interestingly, she went to space again in 1960 and returned home safely once more. Her space travel was commemorated with a special issue Romanian postage stamp in 1959.

Now, her legacy lives on through $Marfusha - the token celebrating this pioneering space explorer.
